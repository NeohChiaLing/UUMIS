A Legacy of Excellence

UUM International School was formed in 2014 to serve the expatriate and local communities of Universiti Utara Malaysia and its surroundings.

UUM International School offers a quality and world-recognized education programme, within a safe and supportive learning environment.

UUM International School is committed to empowering students and staff with equipped skills, knowledge, and competence in becoming successful and responsible citizens in the rapidly changing global world.
